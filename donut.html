Donut Render

This 3D render of a donut and coffee is my first attempt at 3D modeling. Through various YouTube videos navigating me through the basics of Blender, I was able to achieve the final image, along with an animation of it. In Blender, I utilized several modeling brushes, texture wraps, modifiers, nodes, lighting, and animation to create my final render. I am still actively working to improve my skills in Blender and 3D rendering.
