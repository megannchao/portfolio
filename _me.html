_ME

I wanted to create an eerie type of piece. The video utilizes 3 different fake vintage style TV's that have a video of different Adobe Aero projects I created. If you look closely, the bottom left one contains a video of myself with an "x" over both of my eyes. The one on the right is a video of myself with my eyes bleeding. The one in the middle is a collage of the other two as well as a new one of me with ventriloquize makeup. The red lighting over the video was created for a scarier tone, but the purple background was to add a more vintage feel. The music and sound effects I chose also were to add to the scary vibe. This video is a bit reminiscent of The Ring where there is a girl in a TV. The video sequence came together nicely and I think the video turned out to look like an interesting “found footage” type of video.
